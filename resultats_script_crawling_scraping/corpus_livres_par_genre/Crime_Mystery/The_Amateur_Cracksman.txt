The Amateur Cracksmanis an 1899short storycollection byE. W. Hornung. It was published in the UK byMethuen & Co., London, and in the US byScribner's, New York.[1]Many later editions (T. Nelson & Sons, 1914;University of NebraskaPress, 1976; et al.) expand the title toRaffles: The Amateur Cracksman. Some editions such asPenguin Books, 1948, retitle the collection simply,Raffles.
