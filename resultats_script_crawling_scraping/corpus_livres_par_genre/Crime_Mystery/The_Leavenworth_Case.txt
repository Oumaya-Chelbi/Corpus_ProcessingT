The Leavenworth Case(1878), subtitledA Lawyer's Story, is an Americandetective noveland the first novel byAnna Katharine Green. Set in New York City, it concerns the murder of a retired merchant, Horatio Leavenworth, in his New York mansion. The popular[1]novel introduced the detective Ebenezer Gryce, and was influential in the development of the detective novel. Inher autobiography,Agatha Christiecited it as an influence on her own fiction.[2]
