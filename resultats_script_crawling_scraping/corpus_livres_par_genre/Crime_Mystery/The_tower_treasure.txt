The Tower Treasureis the first volume in the originalHardy Boysseries published byGrosset & Dunlap. The book ranks 55th onPublishers Weekly's All-Time Bestselling Children's Book List for the United States, with 2,209,774 copies sold as of 2001.[1]This book is one of the "Original 10", generally considered by historians and critics of children's literature to be the best examples of all the Hardy Boys, andStratemeyer Syndicate, writing.
