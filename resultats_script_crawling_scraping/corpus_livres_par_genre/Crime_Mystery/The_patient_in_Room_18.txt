The Patient in Room 18is a 1929mystery novelwritten byMignon G. Eberhart. Eberhart's first published novel, it follows the adventures of NurseSarah Keate, who would later appear in six more of Eberhart's works, and became one of the most popular mystery characters of the time.[1]The novel later served as the basis for a 1938 motion picture released byWarner Brothers, with the same title, starringPatric KnowlesandAnn Sheridan.[2]
