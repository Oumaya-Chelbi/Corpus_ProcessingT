The Secret of the Old Millis Volume 3 in the originalHardy Boysseries of mystery books for children and teens published byGrosset & Dunlap. The book ranks 86th onPublishers Weekly's All-Time Bestselling Children's Book List for the United States, with 1,467,645 copies sold by 2001.[1]This book is one of the "Original 10", some of the best examples of the Hardy Boys, andStratemeyer Syndicate, writing.
