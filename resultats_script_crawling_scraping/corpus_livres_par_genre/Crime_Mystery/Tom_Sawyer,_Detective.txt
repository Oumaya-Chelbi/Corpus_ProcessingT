Tom Sawyer, Detectiveis an1896novel byMark Twain. It is a sequel toThe Adventures of Tom Sawyer(1876),Adventures of Huckleberry Finn(1885), andTom Sawyer Abroad(1894).Tom Sawyerattempts to solve a mysterious murder in thisburlesqueof the immensely popular detective novels of the time. LikeAdventures of Huckleberry Finn, the story is told using the first-person narrative voice ofHuck Finn.
