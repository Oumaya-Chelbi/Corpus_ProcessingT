The Dark Eyes Of Londonis acrime novelby the British writerEdgar Wallacewhich was first published in 1924.[1]An unbalanced doctor and his brother murder a series of wealthy men to benefit from theirlife insurancepolicies, using a charity for the blind as a front for their activities. The persistent Inspector Holt ofScotland Yardis soon on their trail. It was based on an earlier short storyThe Croakerswhich Wallace had written.
