The Black Maskis a 1901 short story collection byE. W. Hornung. It was published in the UK byGrant Richards, London, and in the US byScribner's, New York under the titleRaffles: Further Adventures of the Amateur Cracksman.[1]It is the second collection of stories in Hornung's series concerningA. J. Raffles, agentleman thiefin lateVictorianLondon.
