Carry On, Jeevesis a collection of ten short stories byP. G. Wodehouse. It was first published in the United Kingdom on 9 October 1925 byHerbert Jenkins, London, and in the United States on 7 October 1927 byGeorge H. Doran, New York.[1]Many of the stories had previously appeared in theSaturday Evening Post, and some were rewritten versions of stories in the collectionMy Man Jeeves(1919). The book is considered part of theJeeves canon.
