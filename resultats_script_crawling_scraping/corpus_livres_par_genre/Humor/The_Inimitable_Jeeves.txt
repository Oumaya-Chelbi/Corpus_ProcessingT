The Inimitable JeevesbyP. G. Wodehousewas the first of theJeevesnovels, although not originally conceived as a single narrative, beingassembled from a number of short storiesfeaturing the same characters. The book was first published in the United Kingdom byHerbert Jenkins, London, on 17 May 1923 and in the United States byGeorge H. Doran, New York, on 28 September 1923, under the titleJeeves.[1]
