The Devil's Dictionaryis asatiricaldictionary written by American journalistAmbrose Bierce, consisting of common words followed by humorous and satirical definitions. The lexicon was written over three decades as a series of installments for magazines and newspapers. Bierce's witty definitions were imitated and plagiarized for years before he gathered them into books, first asThe Cynic's Word Bookin 1906 and then in a more complete version asThe Devil's Dictionaryin 1911.
