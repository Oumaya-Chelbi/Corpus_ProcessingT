Baron Munchausen(/ˈmʌntʃaʊzən,ˈmʊntʃ-/;[1][2][a]German:[ˈmʏnçˌhaʊzn̩]) is a fictionalGerman noblemancreated by the German writerRudolf Erich Raspein his 1785 bookBaron Munchausen's Narrative of His Marvellous Travels and Campaigns in Russia. The character is loosely based onbaronHieronymus Karl Friedrich Freiherr von Münchhausen.
