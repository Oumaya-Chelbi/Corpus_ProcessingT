The Clicking of Cuthbertis a collection of ten short stories byP. G. Wodehouse, all with a golfing theme. It was first published in the United Kingdom on 3 February 1922 byHerbert Jenkins Ltdof London. It was later published in the United States byGeorge H. Doranof New York on 28 May 1924 under the titleGolf Without Tears.[1]The short stories were originally published in magazines between 1919 and 1922.
