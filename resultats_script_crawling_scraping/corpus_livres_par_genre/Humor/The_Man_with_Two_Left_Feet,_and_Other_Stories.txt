The Man with Two Left Feet, and Other Storiesis a collection ofshort storiesby British authorP. G. Wodehouse, first published in the UK on 8 March 1917 byMethuen & Co., London, and in the US on 1 February 1933 byA. L. Burtand Co., New York.[1]All the stories had previously appeared in periodicals, usuallyThe Strand Magazinein the United Kingdom andThe Red Book MagazineorThe Saturday Evening Postin the United States.
