At the Back of the North Windis a children's book written by Scottish authorGeorge MacDonald. It was serialized in the children's magazineGood Words for the Youngbeginning in 1868 and was published in book form in 1871.  It is a fantasy centered on a boy named Diamond and his adventures with the North Wind. Diamond travels together with the mysterious Lady North Wind through the nights.  The book includes the fairy taleLittle Daylight, which has been pulled out as an independent work or added separately to other collections of his fairy tales.
