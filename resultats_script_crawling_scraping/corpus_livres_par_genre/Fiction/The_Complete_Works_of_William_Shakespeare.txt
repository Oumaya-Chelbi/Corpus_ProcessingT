TheComplete Works of William Shakespeareis the standard name given to any volume containing all the plays and poems ofWilliam Shakespeare. Some editions include several works that were not completely of Shakespeare's authorship (collaborative writings), such asThe Two Noble Kinsmen, which was a collaboration withJohn Fletcher;Pericles, Prince of Tyre, the first two acts of which are likely to have been written byGeorge Wilkins; orEdward III, whose authorship is disputed.
