The Jungle Bookis an 1894 collection of stories by the English authorRudyard Kipling. Most of the characters are animals such asShere Khanthe tiger andBaloothe bear, though a principal character is the boy or "man-cub"Mowgli, who is raised in the jungle by wolves. Most stories are set in a forest inIndia; one place mentioned repeatedly is "Seeonee" (Seoni), in thecentralstate ofMadhya Pradesh.
