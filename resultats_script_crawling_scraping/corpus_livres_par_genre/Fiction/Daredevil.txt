Daredevilis the title of amysterynovel byLeslie Charteriswhich was first published byWard Lockin 1929 (followed by an American edition that same year byThe Crime Club). This was Charteris' fourth full-length novel, and is one of the few full-length books in his canon that does not feature the character ofSimon Templar, alias "The Saint". However, the book does have a connection to the Saint series.
