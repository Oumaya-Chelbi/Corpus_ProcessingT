The Happy Prince and Other Tales(orStories) is a collection ofbedtime storiesfor children byOscar Wilde, first published in May 1888. It contains five stories that are highly popular among children and frequently read in schools: "The Happy Prince," "The Nightingale and the Rose," "The Selfish Giant," "The Devoted Friend," and "The Remarkable Rocket." Theshort storiesare valued for theirmorals, and have been made into animated films.
