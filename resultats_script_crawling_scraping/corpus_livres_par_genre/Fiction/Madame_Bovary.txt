Madame Bovary(/ˈboʊvəri/;[1]French:[madambɔvaʁi]), originally published asMadame Bovary: Provincial Manners(French:Madame Bovary: Mœurs de province[madambɔvaʁimœʁ(s)dəpʁɔvɛ̃s]), is a novel byFrenchwriterGustave Flaubert, published in 1857. The eponymous character lives beyond her means in order to escape the banalities and emptiness of provincial life.
